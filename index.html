How To Use
Your Command Center — quick reference
☀ Morning Ritual
1. Open dashboard → Morning popup auto-shows 8–10:30am
2. Paste Apple Notes / brain dump into text area
3. Upload a screenshot if needed (📷)
4. Add any Faye client updates
5. Note anything for Aaron's queue
6. Set your focus for the day
→ Hit Generate Morning Intake
⊞ Dashboard
• All — see every open task across all categories
• Ops Support — Aaron's day-to-day operational needs
• Strategic — fundraising, LP, IR, CoS initiatives
• Faye — client tasks by family (Doherty, Garcia-Huang, etc)
• Office & Events — vendors, Casino Night, logistics
• Personal — your personal goals and tasks
✦ Agent Queue
Claude scans your email, calendar, and Slack to surface suggested actions — draft emails, add tasks, log delegations, create calendar events.

• Check boxes to select items to execute
• Tap any item to edit before executing
• Execute Selected to run approved actions
• Items in the left rail are a compact preview
⏳ Waiting On
Track anything you're waiting on from others — 645 team, Aaron, Faye families, or personal.

Add items with person, what you're waiting for, and a due date. Overdue items show in red.

Use Smart Sync (left rail) to scan Gmail, Slack, and Calendar for items that may have been resolved.
📋 Quick Capture
The Quick Capture pill sits in the Focus Strip at the top:

1. Type or paste anything into the input
2. Select the category (Ops, Faye, Office, etc.)
3. Hit Parse → — Claude extracts tasks
4. Or tap 📷 to upload a screenshot and have Claude read the tasks from it
◆ IOP — 180-Day Plan
Your Initiative Operating Plan tracks progress toward becoming Chief of Staff.

4 initiatives tracked: GP Enablement, Events, Office Transformation, Loop-Closing.

• Monthly check-in button in the sidebar
• Calendar reminders: 1st Mon each month (self-review + with Aaron)
• Full detail in Goals & IOP tab
🔄 Keeping It Updated
When Claude improves the dashboard, you'll get a new index.html to upload:

1. Go to github.com/tashababiiii/Dashboard
2. Click index.html → three dots → Upload file
3. Drag in new file → Commit changes
4. Live at natasha-daily-dashboard.vercel.app in ~60s
