Capture Methods — Getting Things In
📱 Apple Messages
When someone texts you a task or request
1. Open Messages on your Mac
2. Select + copy the relevant text
3. Paste into Quick Capture (right sidebar, Dashboard tab)
4. Select the right workspace (Faye, Personal, 645, Aaron)
5. Hit Parse →
💡 Don't clean it up — paste the messy thread and Claude figures it out
🔔 Apple Reminders
When you have reminders saved in the app
1. Open Reminders on your Mac
2. Select and copy the reminder text
3. Paste into Quick Capture
4. Tag the workspace + hit Parse →
5. Or type directly into the + Add task bar in any column
💡 Paste multiple reminders at once — one dump, one parse
📝 Apple Notes
For notes with tasks buried inside
1. Open Notes, find the relevant note
2. Copy the content (Cmd+A, Cmd+C)
3. Paste into Morning Intake modal (☀ button, top right) — best for daily dump
4. Or paste into Quick Capture for mid-day additions
💡 Best habit: paste Apple Notes into Morning Intake every morning
💬 Slack Tasks
When tasks land in DMs or mentions
1. Open Morning Intake (☀ button, top right)
2. Find the 💬 Slack Scan section
3. Hit Scan Slack →
4. Claude scans the last 24 hours of your DMs and mentions for action items
5. Review the list, check the ones to add, hit Add selected →
💡 Run this every morning — it catches tasks dropped in Slack overnight or the evening before
📧 Email Triage
When emails land overnight or since last scan
1. Open Morning Intake (☀ button, top right)
2. Find the 📧 Email Scan section
3. Hit Scan Email →
4. Claude scans last 24hrs, excludes Unroll.me marketing
5. Results sorted into 3 buckets: Tasks for me · Waiting On · FYI only
6. Check what's right, hit add for each bucket
💡 Waiting On items go straight to the Waiting On tab — tasks go to your 645 dashboard
Since Faye doesn't export anymore
Option 1 — Screenshot: Screenshot Faye task list → share in Claude chat → Claude reads and adds tasks

Option 2 — Copy + Paste: Select tasks in Faye → paste into Quick Capture → set workspace to Faye → Parse

Option 3 — Morning Intake: Paste new Faye tasks in the Faye section of the morning modal
💡 Include the client name — Claude sorts by family automatically

New Features — Delegation Log & IOP Check-In
📋 Delegation Log
When you hand something off to someone else
1. Click the Delegation Log tab
2. Fill in: what was delegated, to whom, follow-up date, any context
3. Hit Log It
4. When resolved, hit Resolve
5. Overdue delegations turn red and show in the badge count
💡 Use for anything you've asked Aaron, Randi, Faye clients, or vendors to do. This becomes your handoff doc and promotion evidence.
◆ IOP Monthly Check-In
Use at your beginning-of-month Aaron IOP review
1. Click ◆ IOP Check-In button (top right)
2. Answer 4 questions: wins, what's behind, next month's focus, what you need from Aaron
3. Hit Generate my accountability report →
4. Claude gives you a sharp, honest report to walk into Aaron's review with
5. Also check off milestones in the Goals & IOP tab as you complete them
💡 Do this the Friday before your beginning-of-month Aaron IOP review so you walk in prepared.
